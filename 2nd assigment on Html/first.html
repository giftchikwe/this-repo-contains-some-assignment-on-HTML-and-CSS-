Benefits of going to classes on time

2. Participation Opportunities: Being early gives you a chance to ask questions, engage in discussions, and participate more actively, which can enhance learning.

5. Time Management: Regular punctuality helps build discipline and time management skills that are valuable in both academic and professional life.
